Let's talk about us

Drew Jones

OPERATIONS MANAGER

Drew joined CCE in 2020. He has been part of the storm water industry since 2017. In this industry, he has been able to blend his passion for the ocean and construction field together. In 2005, he completed an electrical program at WyoTech, receiving his electrical certification in the state of California. Drew works with Property Managers in keeping their property portfolios in compliance with stormwater laws. As a licensed QSP & an inspector of sediment and erosion control, Drew keeps construction sites in compliance by preventing any runoff reaching the waterways. He oversees CCE operations and confirms that all work meets the CCE gold standard of service. In Drew's off time, he enjoys spending time with his girlfriend Tiffany and their fur babies and training Jiu Jitsu.
